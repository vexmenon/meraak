ABOUT US

Deepi & Surbhi co-founders of Meraak Couture focus on the approach to create one-of-a-kind masterpiece and have a burning desire to constantly evolve & aim to become much more than a destination for timeless Indian couture. They take inspiration from fashion trends around the world & different art forms. The collection showcases pastel shades with hues of timeless favourite reds,blues & greens blending them with abstract patterns like geometric, florals & digital.

WHY CHOOSE US ?

At Meraak Couture we specialize in curating different kinds of silhouettes which include voluminous gowns embellished with traditional & or modern embroidery, elaborate lehengas with crop tops & exquisite sarees. Each design is a unique combination of historical grandeur & contemporary chic. The outfits give an impression of sophistication infused with luxury.
